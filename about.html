About Us

Welcome to Celeste - Vintage Watch, where passion meets craftsmanship in the world of vintage watches. Founded by a group of horology enthusiasts, we are dedicated to preserving and celebrating the artistry of timekeeping through carefully curated collections of timeless pieces.


Our journey began with a shared love for vintage watches, each carrying its own unique story and heritage. We believe that every watch is not just a tool for telling time, but a piece of history that reflects the craftsmanship and innovation of its era. Our mission is to connect collectors, enthusiasts, and newcomers with authentic vintage timepieces that evoke nostalgia and elegance.


At Celeste, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and authenticity. Our team of experts meticulously inspects, authenticates, and restores each watch, ensuring that you receive a piece that not only looks stunning but also operates flawlessly. We are passionate about sharing our knowledge and expertise with our customers, guiding you in finding the perfect watch that resonates with your personal style and story.


Join us in exploring the rich tapestry of horological history, and let us help you find your next treasured timepiece.
